LDPE Film Agglomeration: Parameters, Setup, Typical Mistakes

Date: April 24, 2024 | Author: Ihor Kryazhev

LDPE and LLDPE film waste has a bulk density of 30-80 kg/m³. Agglomeration densifies the film to 450-550 kg/m³ through friction and heating. Without this step, pelletizing is impossible. Agglomerator: chamber with 2-4 rotating knives, stator, friction heating system. Cycle 4-12 minutes. At the end — water injection. Clean stretch film: heat to 120-130°C in 4-6 minutes, water 1-2%, density 500-550 kg/m³. Contaminated agricultural film: heat to 130-140°C in 8-12 minutes, water 2-3%, density 450-500 kg/m³. Temperature control with infrared pyrometer.
