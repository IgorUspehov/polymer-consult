Recycled Polyethylene in the EU: Complete Market Overview for LDPE and HDPE

Date: April 23, 2024 | Author: Ihor Kryazhev, Technology Consultant

1. Introduction

The European market for recycled polyethylene (LDPE and HDPE) is moving towards mandatory use of recyclates. By 2030, PPWR requires 30% secondary raw material in packaging. For a technology consultant, knowledge of EuCertPlast certification, deodorization of food-grade recyclates, and tax incentives is the key to reducing scrap by 20% and switching to 100% secondary raw materials.

2. Feedstock and ex-works prices Germany 2024

Clear food-grade LDPE (EFSA) is obtained from industrial stretch film. Price: €1020-1100/t. Black technical LDPE from agricultural film: €620-740/t.

Natural food-grade HDPE (EFSA) comes from Persil/Fairy bottles. REMONDIS in Lünen has introduced vacuum degassing. Price: €980-1150/t. Black technical HDPE: €700-820/t.

3. Largest recyclers with certification

  • mtm plastics (Niedergebra) — 75,000 t/year LDPE, EuCertPlast No. 2022-084
  • REMONDIS Recycling (Lünen) — 85,000 t/year HDPE, food-grade EFSA recyclate
  • PreZero (Neustadt) — 70,000 t/year mixed PE, RecyClass certificate
  • ALBA Group (Berlin) — first food-grade LDPE for contact with fatty products

4. Laws and tax incentives

PPWR (Article 7): By 2030 at least 30% recyclate in plastic packaging. For PE milk and juice bottles — 25% by 2028.

VerpackG and LUCID registration: Fine up to €200,000 for lack of registration. Packaging without recyclate is subject to 20-50% higher license fees.

Italy, virgin plastic tax: €0.45/kg if packaging contains less than 30% recyclate. Savings when using recyclate: 15-20%.

France, AGEC (bonus-malus): Packaging made of 100% virgin LDPE is subject to a 50% higher fee.

5. Examples of finished products

  • HDPE sewage pipes with 60% recyclate (Pipelife, DIN CERTCO certificate)
  • Persil bottles with 50% PCR HDPE from REMONDIS Lünen
  • Knauf PE laminate underlay with 80% LDPE recyclate — CO₂ savings 2.3 kg/m²

6. Conclusion

Germany remains the technology center for low-frequency niches: HDPE deodorization, LDPE vacuum degassing, and two-stage filtration. Demand for clean fractions of clear LDPE and natural HDPE exceeds supply by 1.5-2 times, which will push prices up 5-10% annually until 2027.

Sources: bvse April 2024, EuCertPlast audits, press releases from REMONDIS, mtm plastics, ALBA.
