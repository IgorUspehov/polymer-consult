# Recycled Polyethylene in the EU: Full Market Review (LDPE/HDPE)

## Introduction
The recycled polyethylene market in the European Union is undergoing transformation. LDPE and HDPE form the backbone of recycling.

## Feedstock
- LDPE: stretch films, bags, agricultural film
- HDPE: canisters, bottles, pipes, caps

## Major Recyclers in the EU
- Germany: ALBA, REMONDIS, PreZero, mtm plastics
- France: Paprec, Veolia, Suez
- Italy: Aliplast, Montello, Sorema

## Production Volumes (2023)
- LDPE regranulate: 1.9 million tonnes
- HDPE regranulate: 2.5 million tonnes
- Germany — 31% of European volume

## Prices (EUR/tonne, Germany, 2024)
- Clear LDPE (food grade): 890–1020
- Dark LDPE (technical): 620–740
- HDPE food grade: 980–1150
- HDPE technical: 700–820

## Laws on Recycled Polymers
- SUP Directive (ban on single-use plastics)
- German Packaging Act (VerpackG)
- PPWR (2030: 30% recyclate in packaging)
- AGEC (France), virgin plastic tax (Italy)

## End Products with Recycled PE
- Trash bags (100% recyclate)
- Sewage pipes (up to 60% HDPE recyclate)
- Laminate underlays (30–70% LDPE)
- Crates and pallets
- Detergent bottles (50% PCR)
- Garden furniture, traffic cones

## Conclusion
The market is shifting from voluntary to mandatory use of recyclates. Demand exceeds supply.
