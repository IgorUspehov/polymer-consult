Stretch Film Agglomeration: Complete Setup Guide

Date: 04/24/2024 | Author: Ihor Kryazhev

1. Why agglomeration is necessary

Stretch film has a bulk density of 30-80 kg/m³. Agglomeration densifies it to 500-550 kg/m³.

2. Parameters for clean stretch film

  • Heat to 120-130°C in 4-6 minutes
  • Water addition 1-2%
  • Density 500-550 kg/m³
  • Output 80-150 kg/h

3. Typical mistakes

  • Agglomerate falls apart: underheating or too little water
  • Black spots: overheating or polymer sticking to knives
